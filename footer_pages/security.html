Security & Reliability

Your Data is Our Top Priority

At YusiBooks, we understand that our customers rely on us as an important part of their business processes and record keeping. We take our responsibilities seriously, and the security and reliability of our systems and your data are our highest priorities.

SOC 2 Certified PCI DSS Level 1 GDPR Compliant ISO 27001

Comprehensive Security Measures

Multi-layered protection to keep your financial data safe and secure

256-bit SSL Encryption

All information traveling between your browser and YusiBooks is protected with industry-standard 256-bit SSL encryption. The lock icon in your browser verifies secure data transmission.

  • End-to-end encryption
  • Secure data in transit
  • Certificate validation

Continuous Monitoring

Our servers are continuously scanned for vulnerabilities by third-party security providers. Any identified issues are addressed immediately to maintain the highest security standards.

  • 24/7 vulnerability scanning
  • Third-party security audits
  • Rapid threat response

Data Center Security

YusiBooks servers are located in state-of-the-art data centers with biometric access controls, constant surveillance, and robust infrastructure protection measures.

  • Biometric access controls
  • 24/7 surveillance
  • Redundant power systems

PCI DSS Compliance

YusiBooks maintains PCI DSS Level 1 compliance for secure payment processing. We handle cardholder data in accordance with the highest industry standards.

  • PCI DSS Level 1 certified
  • Annual third-party audits
  • Secure payment processing

Secure Development

Our development team follows OWASP secure coding practices and principles of least access to ensure application security from the ground up.

  • OWASP best practices
  • Principle of least access
  • Code security reviews

Incident Response

In the event of any security incident, we have comprehensive breach notification procedures to inform authorities and customers within required timeframes.

  • Rapid incident response
  • Legal compliance
  • Customer notification

Unmatched Reliability

Enterprise-grade infrastructure designed for maximum uptime and data protection

Redundant Infrastructure

Our infrastructure uses redundant storage and servers across multiple geographic locations to ensure maximum availability and data protection.

  • Multiple data centers
  • Automatic failover
  • Load balancing

Enterprise Cloud Hosting

YusiBooks utilizes enterprise-grade cloud infrastructure to provide fast feature delivery, continuous innovation, and exceptional reliability.

  • 99.9% uptime guarantee
  • Global content delivery
  • Scalable architecture

Comprehensive Backups

Your data is replicated across multiple database servers and backed up nightly to secure offsite locations to prevent any data loss.

  • Real-time replication
  • Nightly offsite backups
  • Point-in-time recovery

24/7 System Monitoring

Our systems are monitored around the clock to ensure optimal performance and immediate response to any potential issues.

  • Real-time alerting
  • Performance optimization
  • Proactive maintenance
